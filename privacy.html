Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 13, 2024

1. Introduction

TripLogger ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Data

TripLogger uses GPS and motion sensors to automatically detect and track your trips. Location data is:

  • Processed locally on your device
  • Stored securely on our servers for trip history
  • Used only for mileage tracking and trip detection
  • Never sold or shared with third parties

2.2 Account Information

We collect basic account information including:

  • Email address (for account creation and login)
  • Password (encrypted and securely stored)
  • Trip data and expense records you create

2.3 Motion and Fitness Data

We use motion sensors to distinguish between different types of movement (driving, walking, stationary) for accurate trip detection. This data is processed locally on your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Automatically detect and track your trips
  • Calculate mileage and generate IRS-compliant reports
  • Provide expense tracking and management features
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Send important account and service notifications

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure cloud storage with regular backups
  • JWT authentication for secure API access
  • Rate limiting to prevent unauthorized access

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app settings.

6. Third-Party Services

TripLogger uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat - Subscription management and payment processing
  • Apple App Store - In-app purchases and subscriptions

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data (PDF/CSV reports)
  • Opt-out of location tracking (disables automatic trip detection)

8. Children's Privacy

TripLogger is not intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or in-app notification.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@triplogger.app
