Built around the real trip workflow

Capture, review, classify, export.

TripLogger is designed for drivers who need reliable mileage records without babysitting an app while driving. Automatic detection is supported, manual recording remains available, and the data stays easy to review later.

AUTO

Automatic trip detection

Background motion and location monitoring starts recording when a drive is detected, even after app termination.

LIVE

Live trip status

In-progress trip metrics are available in app and can surface on the lock screen / widget path on supported iOS setups.

MAP

Route + timeline review

See the recorded route, inspect trip details, and edit before saving so your log stays clean.

YTD

Fast yearly summaries

Year-to-date totals load instantly from local cache while deeper chart data loads on demand.

IRS

IRS mileage support

Mileage rates by year, deduction calculations, and report exports help prepare for tax filing and accounting workflows.

EXP

Expense + transaction tracking

Keep fuel, tolls, parking, income, and related records in the same place as your trips.

How the flow works

Designed to reduce taps while driving.

The ideal path is simple: enable permissions once, let trips get detected, review what matters later, then export when needed.

1

Set up once

Sign in, choose your vehicle, enable location and motion permissions, and turn on auto trip monitoring.

2

Drive normally

TripLogger detects movement, starts recording in the background, and maintains trip state for later recovery.

3

Review and classify

Open the app, confirm route details, mark business/personal, and add notes or expenses if needed.

4

Export when needed

Generate clean reports and share CSV/PDF exports with your accountant or back-office workflows.
